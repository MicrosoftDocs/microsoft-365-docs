# Secure Windows 10 PCs

> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/videoplayer/embed/RE4kcAF?autoplay=false]
 
## Try it!  

After you set up device protection in Microsoft 365 Business, follow these steps to protect your Windows 10 computers.

1. In the Microsoft 365 admin center, choose  **Setup**.
2. Under  **Secure your Windows computers** , select  **View**.
3. Review the information on the  **Secure your Windows 10 computers**  page so that you understand the policies and how they affect your users.
4. Select  **Get started**.
5. On the  **Windows 10 settings**  pane, review the options and adjust if needed. Hover over the tooltip to learn more about a policy.
6. Under  **Who should the policy apply to?** , choose whether your selections will be applied to everyone in your organization or to specific security groups.
7. Select  **Save changes**.

Microsoft 365 Business is now helping to protect your Windows 10 computers.
