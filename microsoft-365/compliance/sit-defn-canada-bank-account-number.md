# Canada bank account number

## Format

7 or 12 digits

## Pattern

A Canada Bank Account Number is 7 or 12 digits.

A Canada bank account transit number is:

- five digits
- a hyphen
- three digits
OR
- a zero "0"
- eight digits

## Checksum

No

## Definition
